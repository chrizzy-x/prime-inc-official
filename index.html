The Story

From Lagos, building global infrastructure.

Prime Inc started with a question: why does AI infrastructure assume you have reliable power, a stable internet connection, and a bank account? Nobody building infrastructure was building from Lagos. So Riz decided to. Bootstrapped, solo, no outside capital — every product ships because the work demands it.

Why Lagos

The world's most honest product environment.

Lagos is one of the most competitive, resource-constrained, high-velocity cities on earth. Intermittent power means Prime Gadgets exists. Variable connectivity means Prime Pay's USSD layer exists. Building here forces honesty that a comfortable environment never would.

The Conviction

The AI economy needs new primitives.

Not AI wrappers on top of 2010s SaaS. Real primitives — agent coordination, verified identity, permissionless finance, payment rails for both humans and machines. AgentOS is the agent layer. FFP is the settlement layer. Prime Pay is the access layer. The goal isn't to compete with Silicon Valley. The goal is to build the stack that makes the next generation of companies possible — from anywhere.
